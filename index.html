HIIT

HIIT stands for High-Intensity Interval Training. It is a form of cardiovascular exercise that involves alternating between short bursts of intense activity and periods of rest or low-intensity activity. This type of training is typically performed for a short duration, usually 20-30 minutes, but it is designed to be very challenging and effective at burning calories and improving cardiovascular fitness.

BOXING

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, wearing gloves, punch each other in a boxing ring for a predetermined amount of time or until one of them is unable to continue. The sport requires a combination of physical and mental skill, strength, speed, agility, endurance, and strategy. Boxers typically train in various techniques to improve their punching ability, defense, and overall physical fitness.

AEROBICS

Aerobics is a type of physical exercise that involves rhythmic, repetitive movements that aim to increase cardiovascular endurance and overall fitness. Typically, aerobics involves performing exercises to music, with the goal of maintaining a continuous, elevated heart rate for an extended period of time. The exercises can be low-impact or high-impact, depending on the individual's fitness level and preference.
